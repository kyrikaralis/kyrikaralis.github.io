My pottery is about line, form, and function. My work is intended to be used daily. I believe that using handmade objects can bring a newness, a specialness, to every day rituals such as drinking your morning coffee; it's this notion that keeps me interested in creating functional pottery. I have sold my work through Etsy since 2007, and currently I have works for sale through this website, and in person at local markets.

About me:
I grew up reading and daydreaming in a sleepy Central Florida beach town, and I fell in love with music theory, singing and visual art in high school. I have my BFA in photography from the University of North Florida in 2004.

In addition to making pottery, I take photographs, knit, ride and work on bicycles, hike, SUP, practice yoga and generally spend as much time outdoors as possible.
