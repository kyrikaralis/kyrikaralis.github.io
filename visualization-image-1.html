Click Through Rate vs. Ad Spend

Click Through Rate vs. Ad Spend shows any correlation between how much one spends on a particular ad, as compared to the Click Through Rate, which Facebook Ads Manager reports as how many times an ad link is clicked. This does not count the number of unique peole who click on the ad. Our Click Through Rate goal is .87%. There isn't a super direct correlation here, and part of this could be due to the fact that we asked ads to perform in three different ways throughout the year, or asked them to optimize for different things. This also doesn't take into effect ad copy (words & photos), or to whom the ad was directed, or how large a population there is in a geographic location that the ad was directed to. Lots of variables!
