Social Media Ad Effectiveness

The purpose of this project is to take real data from Facebook ads aired in 2018, and observe correlations between what happened at the ad-level: ad spend, cost per click, click through rate and ad optimization, versus the fill rate and revenue generated of the classes the ads were aired for.

Two datasets were used to compile this report. One came from Facebook Ads Manager. The second report came from the Event Registration tool where we gathered fill rates and an estimation of revenue generated for each class.
